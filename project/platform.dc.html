Invest

Fractional access, done the regulated way.

Where fractional ownership is offered, it's only through SEBI-registered SM REIT partners, with units that can be resold on-exchange. We never pool money into a scheme of our own, and we never hold your title against a promised payout.

You always see who the registered entity is, and you transact with them directly.

RISK DISCLOSURE
  • Returns are not guaranteed. Past performance is not indicative.
  • Property can sit vacant; income may pause or fall.
  • Fractional units can be illiquid — you may not be able to sell when you want.
  • You can lose capital. Invest only via the registered entity's own documents.
